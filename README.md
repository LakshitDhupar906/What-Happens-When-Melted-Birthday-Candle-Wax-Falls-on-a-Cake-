# Birthday Candle Wax on Cakes: A Scientific Review of Food Safety and Health Implications

## Abstract
Birthday candles are a universal tradition, yet the melted wax that falls onto cakes is rarely questioned from a food safety perspective. This paper reviews the composition of common candle waxes, examines their potential health effects when accidentally consumed, and evaluates the overall risk based on available scientific literature and toxicological evidence.

## About
This repository contains my independent research paper reviewing the safety of melted birthday candle wax that drips onto cakes during celebrations.

The work summarizes available scientific information regarding:
- Common candle wax ingredients
- Human toxicity
- Digestibility
- Food contamination
- Potential health risks
- Safety recommendations
